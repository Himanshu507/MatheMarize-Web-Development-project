ELLIPSE QUESTIONS

Question 1: An ellipse has OB as semi minor axis, F and F' its focii and the angle FBF' is a right angle. Then the eccentricity of the ellipse is-

Answer : \(1\over \sqrt{2}\)

Question 2: In an ellipse, the distance between its foci is 6 and minor axis is 8. Then its eccentricity is-

Answer : \(3\over 5\)

Question 3: A focus of an ellipse is at origin. The directrix is the line x = 4 and the eccentricity is \(1\over 2\). Then the length of the semi-major axis is-

Answer : \(8\over 3\)

Question 4: The equation of the circle passing through the foci of the ellipse \(x^2\over 16\) + \(y^2\over 9\) = 1 and having centre at (0,3) is :

Answer : \(x^2+y^2-6y-7\) = 0

Question 5: Equation of the line passing through the point of intersection of parabola \(x^2\) = 8y and the ellipse \(x^2\over 3\) + \(y^2\) = 1 is-

Answer : 3y-1=0

Question 6: If the curve \(x^2\over {\alpha}^2\) + \(y^2\over 4\) = 1 and \(y^3\) = 16x intersect at right angles, then the value of \(\alpha\) is :

Answer : \(4\over 3\)

Question 7: A point on the ellipse, \(4x^2+9y^2\) = 36, where the normal is parallel to the line, 4x-2y-5=0, is :

Answer :(\(9\over 5\), \(8\over 5\))

Question 8: The locus of the foot of perpendicular drawn from the centre of the ellipse \(x^2+3y^2\) = 6 on any tangent to it is :

Answer :\({(x^2+y^2)}^2\) = \(6x^2 + 2y^2\)

Question 9: The area of the quadrilateral formed by the tangents at the end points of the latus recta to the ellipse \(x^2\over 9\) + \(y^2\over 5\) = 1 :

Answer :27
