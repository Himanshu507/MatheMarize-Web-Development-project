PARABOLA QUESTIONS

Question 1: The equation of a tangent to the parabola \(y^2\) = 8x is y = x + 2. The point on this line from which the other tangent to the parabola is perpendicular to the given tangent is -

Answer : (-2, 0)

Question 2: A parabola has the origin as its focus and the line x = 2 as the directrix. Then the vertex of the parabola is at -

Answer : (1, 0)

Question 3: If two tangents drawn from a point P to the parabola \(y^2\) = 4x are at right angles then the locus of P is :-

Answer : x = -1

Question 4: The point of intersection of the normals to the parabola \(y^2\) = 4x at the ends of its latus rectum is -

Answer : (3, 0)

Question 5: The slope of a line touching both, the parabolas \(y^2\) = 4x and \(x^2\) = -32y is :

Answer : \(1\over 2\)

Question 6: Let O be the vertex and Q be any point on the parabola \(x^2\) = 8y. If the point P divides the line segment OQ internally in the ratio 1 : 3, then the locus of P is :-

Answer : \(x^2\) = 2y

Question 7: The locus of the vertices of the family of parabola y = \(a^3x^2\over 3\) + \(a^2x\over 2\) - 2a is -

Answer : xy = \(105\over 64\)
