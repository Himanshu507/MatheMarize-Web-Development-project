STATISTICS QUESTIONS

Question 1: The mean of Mathematics marks of 100 students of a class is 72. If the number of boys is 70 and the mean of their marks is 75. Then the mean of the marks of girls in the class will be-

Answer : 65

Question 2: The median of a set of 9 distict observations is 20.5. If each of the largest four observations of the set is increased by 2, then the median of the new set-

Answer : remain the same as that of original set

Question 3: In a frequency distribution, the mean and median are 21 and 22 respectively, then its mode is approximately

Answer : 24

Question 4: The mean of the numbers a, b, 8, 5, 10 is 6 and the variance is 6.80 then which of the following gives possible values of a and b?

Answer : a = 3, b = 4

Question 5: If the mean deviation of the numbers 1, 1 + d, 1 + 2d, ....., 1 + 100d from their mean is 255, then that d is equal to-

Answer : 10.1

Question 6: From two data sets each of size is 5, the variances are given to be 4 and 5 and the corresponding means are given to be 2 and 4 respectively, then the variance of the combined data set is-

Answer : \(11\over 2\)

Question 7: If the mean deviation about the median of the numbers a, 2a, ......., 50a is 50, then |a| equals-

Answer : 4

Question 8: A scientist is weighing each of 30 fishes. Their mean weight worked out is 30 gm and a standard deviation of 2 gm. Later it was found that the measuring scale was misaligned and always under reported every fish weight by 2 gm. The correct mean and standard deviation (in gm) of fishes are respectively :

Answer : 32, 2

Question 9: The mean of the data set comprising of 16 observations is 16. If one of the observation valued 16 is deleted and three new observations valued 3, 4 and 5 are added to the data, then the mean of the resultant data, is :

Answer : 14
